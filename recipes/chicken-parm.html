Chicken Parmesan

This is the recipe for Chicken parmesan. This is an easy chicken Parm recipe that's delicious. You can do this in an oven or a toaster oven.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 package skinless, bonless chicken breasts
  • 4 slices mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups tomato sauce, or to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 190° C. Spray a baking pan with cooking spray. Place chicken on the prepared pan.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes, flippin chicken after 12 minutes. Remove from the oven, add desired amount of sauce, and top each breast with mozzarella cheese.
  3. Bake until cheese is melted and chicken is lo longer pink in the center and the juices jun clear, about 12 more minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 74° C.
